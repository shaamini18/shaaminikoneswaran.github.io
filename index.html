What is Kollywood?

Kollywood is essentially Tamil cinema where movies are produced in the Tamil language. Just like in India, the movie production industry as a whole is called Bollywood where Hindi is the primary language that movies are produced in. Kollywood is a very diverse community with films produced in multiple langauges.
