S. P. Balasubrahmanyam

SPB was born on June 4, 1946. His full name is Sripathi Panditaradhyula Balasubrahmanyam. He also goes by SPB, (most common) SP Balu, or Balu. He is a playback singer, actor, music composer, film producer, dubbing artist, and television presenter. SPB is well known as the greatest Indian singer of all time. He mostly sang songs in Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, and Hindi. Sripathi Panditaradhyula Balasubrahmanyam sang in 16 different languages. Some of his songs are “Sundari Kannal”, “Sempoove Poove”, “Kaadhal Rojave”, etc.

Ilayaraaja

Ilaiyaraaja was born on June 3, 1943. Ilaiyaraaja’s birth name was R. Gnanathesikan. He is a musician, conductor, composer, lyricist, singer and can play multiple instruments. He has composed over 7000 songs and has performed in 20,000 concerts. He holds the title of “Isaignani” (musical genius). Ilayaraaja was married to Jeeva and they had three children. All his kids are singers and film composers. One of his sons named Yuvan Shankar Raja is a famous composer. Some of his songs are “Kaattumalli”, “Raja Raja Chozhan”, “Valaiyosai”, “Sangeetha Megam”, etc.

Anirudh Ravichander

Anirudh Ravichander was born on October 16, 1990. Anirudh is a singer, instrumentalist, music composer, and music producer. Anirudh makes music mostly for Tamil movies but has for some Telugu films too. He is the son of actor Ravi Raghavendra and he is the nephew of Rajinikanth. Anirudh has won many awards throughout his career and will receive more. Some of his songs are “Why This Kolaveri Di”, “Kanave Kanave”, “Enna Solla”, “Thenmozhi”, “Nee Paartha Vizhigal”, “Jimikki Ponnu”, etc.

Hariharan

Hariharan was born on April 3, 1955. His full name is Hariharan Anantha Subramani. He is an Indian playback singer and has sung mostly in Tamil, Hindi, and Telugu. Hariharan has sung more than 15,000 songs in 10 different languages. Hariharan is the son of HAS Mani and Alamelu Mani. His father was a Carnatic singer and he taught many. Unfortunately, he died from cardiac arrest in his early 40’s. His mother was a Carnatic singer and a teacher. Some of his songs are “Vaaney Vaaney”, “Kannulo Unnavu”, “Thodu Vaanam”, “En Jeevan”, “Venmegam”.

D.Imman

D.Imman was born on January 24, 1983. D.Imman’s full name is Immanuel Vasanth Dinakaran. He is a singer, and film composer and he worked mostly in Tamil cinema. He has made music for more than 100 films. D.Imman started his career making music in films at the age of 15. D.Imman is a music director, playback singer, record producer and a composer. Some of D.Imman’s compositions are “OnnuVitta Yarum Yenakila”, “Nanbiye”, “Kurumdba”, “Koodamela Koodavechi”, “Marudhaani”, “Un Mele Oru Kannu”.

A.R. Rahman

A.R. Rahman whose birth name was Dileep Kumar was born on January 6,1967. Allah Rakha Rahman is a well-known Indian music composer, record producer, singer, songwriter, musician, multi-instrumentalist, and philanthropist. He is best known for his work in Indian cinema, primarily in Tamil and Hindi films. He won two Grammy awards and 6 National Film Awards. A.R. Rahman is married to Saira Banu and they have three children. Some of his songs are “Ponni Nadhi”, “Aga Naga”, “Singappenney”, “Azhagiye”, “Muzumathi”, “Anbil Avan”, etc.

S. Janaki

S.Janaki was born on April 23, 1938. Her full name is Sistla Janaki. She is a playback singer from Andhra Pradesh. She holds the title “Nightingale of South India” and people call her “Janaki Amma” in respect to her. S.Janaki is one of the best playback singers of all time. Janaki Amma has sung 48,000 songs for films, radio, albums, etc. Her career spanned over 60 years and she created some outstanding pieces. She sang in 17 different languages and is well-known as the “Queen of expressions''. Some of her songs are “Thendrale Thendrale”, “Amma Amma”, “Chinna Chinna Vanna Kuyil”, “Malare”, etc.

Chitra

Chithra’s full name is Krishnan Nair Shantakumari Chithra. She was born on July 27, 1963. Chithra is a carnatic and playback singer. She has sung more than 18,000 songs in the duration of her career. She has sung songs in many languages including foreign languages such as Latin, English and French too. She is married to Vijayashankar who is an engineer and a businessman. They had a daughter but unfortunately, she drowned in a pool in Dubai where Chithra was going to perform in a concert with A.R. Rahman. Some of her songs are “Soul Of Varisu”, “Veera Raja Veera”, “Konji Pesida Venaam”, “Kannamoochi Yenada”, “Innisai Paadivarum”, etc.
