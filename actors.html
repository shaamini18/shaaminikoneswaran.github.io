Thalapathy Vijay

Vijay was born on June 22, 1974, in Madras, Tamil Nadu. His full name is Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar. His father is a Tamil film director and his mother is a playback singer and carnatic vocalist. Vijay had a sister but she died at the age of 2 years old. Vijay holds the title “Ilaya Thalapathy '' (Young Commander) in the industry. Vijay was a child actor and he had acted in movies with the famous Rajinikanth. Then he took on lead roles and his first movie was Naalaiya Theerpu in 1992.

Vijay is my favorite actor of all time. He can dance like no other even to this day. He has created such impressive movies such as “Ghilli”, “Theri”, “Poove Unakkaga”, “Sachein”, “Pokkiri”, “Aathi”, “Kushi”, “Vaseegara", and etc. Not only can he dance well but his acting skills are on top which explains why everyone in the South likes him. He is all in one actor, he can dance, sing, act, and he is a respectful man.

Trisha Krishnan

Trisha Krishnan was born on May 4, 1983. Trisha lives with her mother and grandmother as her father sadly died in 2012. Trisha can speak Tamil, Hindi, French, and English. The majority of the films she has acted in are Tamil and Telugu films. In 1999, she won the Miss Chennai title. After this, she made her entrance into the film industry. She is referred to as the “ Queen of South India '' of her versatile appearance in all film industries of the South Indian Languages. She was also ranked the best actress by The Times Of India. Some of Trisha’s films are “Ghilli”, “96”, “Aathi”, “Aaru”, “Ponniyan Selvan 1&2”, etc.

Suriya

Suriya was born on July 23, 1975. Saravanan Sivakumar is his birth name and his stage name is Suriya which is commonly used to identify him. Suriya is an actor in Tamil Cinema and he is one of the highest-earning actors. Suriya had a brother named Karthi and a sister named Brindha. Karthi, the younger brother, is also in the film industry. Suriya is married to Jyothika who is an actress in Tamil Cinema. She had made some outstanding films, one of which was with Suriya called “Kaakha Kaakha”. They have two children ages 12 and 15 years old. Some of Suriya’s films are “Kaakha kaakha”, “Aaru”, “Anjaan”, “Sillunu Oru Kaadhal”, “24”, “Vaaranam Aayiram”, etc.

Dhanush

Dhanush was born on July 28, 1983. His birth name is Venkatesh Prabhu Kasthuri Raja. Dhanush is an actor, director, lyricist, product, and playback singer. Dhanush has acted in 46 films and has won multiple awards as an actor and producer. He has a brother named Selvaraghavan and he is a director who pressured Dhanush to become an actor. Selvaraghavan starred Dhanush in multiple of his movies. Dhanush married Aishwarya who is Rajinikanth’s daughter and they had two children named Yatra and Linga. Unfortunately, they had a divorce in 2022. SOme of his films would be “VIP 1 & 2”, “Thanga Magan”, “Anegan”, “Maari 1 & 2”, “Asuran”, “Uthama Puthiran”, etc.

Vikram

Vikram was born on April 17, 1966. Vikram’s birth name is Kennedy John Victor. Vikram has acted in the majority of his films in Tamil cinema. Vikram plays many roles and has won many awards throughout his career. Vikram’s father, John Victor, started a career in films and played minor roles. Vikram’s mother, Rajeshwari, is a government officer with the position of sub-collector. Vikram had two siblings, a brother named Arvind and a sister named Anitha, who is a teacher. Some of his movies are “Raavanan”, “Ponniyin Selvan 1 & 2”, “Deiva Thirumagal”, “Anniyan”, “Iru Mugan”, “David”, “Saamy”, etc.

Nayanthara

Nayanthara’s birth name is Diana Marian Kurian. She was born on November 18, 1984. Nayanthara appears mostly in Tamil, Malayalam, and Telugu films but she also has done Kannada and Hindi movies. Nayanthara is one of the highest-earning actresses in India. She has acted in 80+ movies in the duration of 20 years. She is well known as Lady Superstar in Indian cinema. Nayanthara married Vignesh Shivan, a director; they had two children in 2022. Some movies of hers are “Yaaradi Nee Mohini”, “Viswasam”, “Raja Rani”, “Mr.Local”, “Annaaethe”, “Villa”, etc.

Simran

Simran’s birth name is Rishibala Naval. She was born on April 4, 1976. Simran mostly acted in the Tamil movie industry. Simran also did Telugu, Hindi, Malayalam. Kannada films too. She was a singer, dancer, producer, and actress. Simran is one of the most versatile actresses who has played many roles throughout her career. She has 3 siblings, 2 sisters named Monal and Jyothi and 1 brother named Surneet. She married her childhood friend, Deepak Bagga, and has 2 kids with him. Some of her movies are “Thulladha Manamum Thullum”, “Priyamanavale”, “Kannathil Muthamittal”, etc.
